Cyber forensics is the emerging technology and is expected to play a major role in the lives of human beings. It is expected that by 2020 there will be more than 50 billion devices connected to the internet.

This huge volume of devices will naturally lead to a major issue with security, and it becomes very essential to have a strong secure network built around these devices to prevent crackers from compromising systems and it’s essential to make a forensic way to trace the problems.

Unlike comprising banks and credit cards, weak Network systems can compromise entire cities and also defense, or infrastructure.

It is very essential that we need to develop a strong force that will safeguard our cyber world related development from crackers.
